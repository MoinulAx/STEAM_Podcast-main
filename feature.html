Meet The Crew

Podcast101 is hosted in the Brooklyn Steam Center with the purpose to give the students their own voice. The moto being work with not for. There are two parts of the podcasting program being the creative team and the technical team.

Our Purpose

The purpose of the Podcast101 is to allow scholars to learn how to voice their opinion and truly learn what they want to do in the future. All students from any path way is allowed into the following program with students getting hands on experience by getting experience in external events as well.

Partners

Scholars have had been opposites with partners of the Podcast101 getting real hands on work experience in the AV field, working in many events which they used all skill that was used in the podcast technical part. Some examples would be Nanotronics and G-Factor.
